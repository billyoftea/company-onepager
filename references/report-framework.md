# Unified Report Framework

Use the same reporting lens across A-shares, HK-listed names, and US-listed names.

## Output sections

1. 公司
2. 业务
3. 财务摘要
4. 亮点
5. 风险
6. 一句话结论

## What `业务` should do

Write this as a finished report paragraph or bullet set, not an instruction to the reader.

Always try to cover these angles:
- What the company actually sells or provides
- Where revenue or profit is mainly coming from
- Industry or platform positioning
- Business concentration when it matters

Avoid meta phrases such as:
- “可以先把它放进…”
- “适合继续研究…”
- “可进一步结合…”

Prefer direct report language instead.

## What `财务摘要` should emphasize

Prioritize low-frequency, decision-useful data:
- Revenue
- Revenue growth
- Gross margin
- Net income
- Net income growth
- Net margin
- Operating cash flow
- Balance-sheet leverage
- Market cap
- Valuation multiples such as PE, PB, or PS

Do not prioritize high-frequency trading fields such as:
- Latest price
- Open price
- Intraday range
- Same-day percentage move

## Interpretation rules

- Translate raw data into a view, not a data dump
- Keep tone concise and final-report-like
- Treat `亮点` as the current investment case
- Treat `风险` as the main reasons the thesis can weaken
- Make `一句话结论` sound like a verdict, not a suggestion or next-step prompt
